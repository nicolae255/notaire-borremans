Notarial public auction

How does a public auction work?

The notarial public auction is organised under the authority of the notary. It offers complete transparency and maximum legal security for both sellers and buyers.

Since 2020, bidding takes place primarily on the Biddit platform, enabling online participation from anywhere in Belgium.

Entrust my property
01
Property valuation

Our office establishes a base price consistent with the local property market.

02
Publication on Biddit and viewings

The property is listed on Biddit and partner sites. Viewings are arranged before the auction date.

03
Award session

Bids are opened online via Biddit. The notary awards the property to the highest bidder. The award is immediate with no withdrawal period.

04
Authentic deed

Our office drafts and authenticates the deed of sale within the period specified in the terms of sale (generally 6 weeks).

Private sale through a notary

How does a private sale work?

A private sale is arranged by the notary between a seller and a buyer. No auction — just comprehensive support from valuation to handover of keys.

Our office handles the entire process: property assessment, organisation of viewings, drafting of deeds and legal security of the transaction.

Entrust my property
01
Property valuation

Our office assesses your property and advises you on the right selling price.

02
Promotion through the right channels

Your property is advertised through appropriate channels to reach potential buyers.

03
Organising viewings

We organise viewings with interested buyers and guide you through to the selection of an offer.

04
Preliminary agreement

Signing of the preliminary agreement drafted by the notary. The transaction is legally secured from this point onward.

05
Authentic deed

Signing of the deed of sale before the notary. Handover of keys to the new owner.
